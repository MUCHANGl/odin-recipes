Boiled eggs.

Description.

When boiling eggs, you have to pay attention to the amount of time the eggs are in the boiling water because different timings produces different types of boiled eggs.

Ingredients and tools.

  • Eggs.
  • Pot.
  • Water.

Steps.

  1. Heat up a pot of water until the water starts to boil.
  2. Gently place the eggs in the boiling water.
  3. 6 minutes gets a runny yolk and bearly set whites.
  4. 8 minutes gets a soft boiled yolk with a soft-set white.
  5. 10 minutes gets a firm whites with fully cooked yolk.
  6. 15 miniutes brings out a rubbery whites and dry yolks.
