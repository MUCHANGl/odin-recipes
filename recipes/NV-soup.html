Boiled Noodles and Vegetable Soup.

Description.

This meal is very easy to cook, takes almost no time at all and tastes delicious! Sometimes I like having it with soft boiled eggs that are runny and soft.

Ingredients

  • Noodles.
  • Large onions.
  • Green peppers.
  • Persly.
  • Tomatoes.
  • Knorr cubes.
  • Ginger powder.
  • Garlic powder.
  • Black peppers.
  • Tomato paste.

Steps.

  1. Dice your vegetables and set them aside separately.
  2. In a pre-heated pot, cook the diced onions to golden brown.
  3. In the same pot, put the diced green peppers for 2 minutes on medium heat.
  4. Now the rest of the vegetables are to be put in the pot.
  5. Add 2 cups of water to the cooked vegetables and stir for 1 minute and wait for it to boil.
  6. Place the noodles in the boiling soup and the spices and once the noodles detach, start stirring while adding a pinch of salt.
  7. Serve it hot!
  8. Now enjoy your meal!
